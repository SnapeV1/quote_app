# 📱 DailyLine

**DailyLine** is a personal iOS/Android application designed to help you rediscover and remember meaningful book quotes you’ve collected over time.

Instead of letting your favorite lines fade into memory, DailyLine brings them back to you—one quote at a time, every day.

---

## ✨ Features

* 📚 **Save Your Favorite Quotes**

  * Store quotes along with:

    * Author
    * Book title
  * Build your own personal library of meaningful lines

* 🌅 **Daily Quote Delivery**

  * Get one quote from your collection every day
  * A simple way to stay inspired and connected to your readings

* 🧩 **Home Screen Widget**

  * View your daily quote directly from your device’s home screen
  * No need to open the app

* 🔍 **Quote Collection**

  * Browse all saved quotes anytime
  * Clean and minimal interface for easy reading

---

## 🎯 Purpose

DailyLine is a **personal project** built to solve a simple problem:

> *We read great books, highlight amazing quotes… and then forget them.*

This app ensures that your favorite ideas, phrases, and insights stay with you—daily.
